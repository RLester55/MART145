How to play League of Legends

This website will include all the basics to understand the popular videogame, League of Legends

Overview

League of Legends is a 5v5 competetive multiplayer game released by Riot Games in 2009

It features a wide variety of characters to choose from, releasing a new one about once every 8 months

The game takes place on a square map consisting of both teams bases in the bottem left and top right corners. 3 Lanes connect the two bases. The lanes are at the top edge, middle, and bottom edge of the square map. In between the lanes is the jungle.

Each of the 5 players are assigned to a specific role on the team. Top lane, Jungle, Mid lane, Bot lane, and Support
